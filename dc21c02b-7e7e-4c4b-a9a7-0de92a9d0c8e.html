Hang out and enjoy various games at the Plaza! The Plaza is a massive place filled with activities and games that can all be enjoyed in one place. It's like a virtual theme park filled with multiplayer games. Everything in the Plaza is multiplayer so you can participate with all the activities with your friends or make new friends!
